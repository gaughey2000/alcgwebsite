About

Adam Little Combat Gym, built for disciplined training.

Based in Kirkham and serving the wider Lancashire area, Adam Little Combat Gym is a dedicated combat sports facility built on discipline, respect, and hard work.

Our focus

High-quality coaching with safety at the core.

Every session emphasizes correct technique, respect for training partners, and steady personal development inside and outside the gym.

  • High-quality coaching focused on discipline, respect, and hard work.
  • Safety-first training with correct technique and smart progression.
  • Welcoming culture for complete beginners through to experienced fighters.
  • Strong community and a supportive team atmosphere on and off the mats.
  • Personal development that builds confidence, fitness, and real combat skills.

Ideal for anyone looking to improve fitness, build confidence, learn real combat skills, and train in a structured, professional environment.
